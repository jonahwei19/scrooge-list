Methodology (v3)

The Scrooge Score (0-100) measures how much a billionaire is undergiving relative to their capacity. Higher scores indicate more Scrooge-like behavior.

Formula: Score = 100 × (1 - Giving Ratio)

Where Giving Ratio = Total Lifetime Giving / (Net Worth × Liquidity Factor)

Key Distinctions

  • DISBURSED, not pledged — Only money that has actually left their control
  • EXTERNAL, not self-controlled — DAF transfers to their own DAF don't count
  • GRANTS, not assets — A $10B foundation that grants $50M/year is $50M of giving

Data Confidence

HIGH: 990-PF filings, Foundation reports
MEDIUM: News coverage, University announcements
LOW: Estimates, incomplete data
