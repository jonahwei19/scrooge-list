A Note on Method

The Scrooge Score tallies what a person has given against what they could plausibly have given. It draws from 990-PF foundation filings, SEC Form 4 securities transfers, FEC political donations, the official Giving Pledge register, and eleven further categories of public record. Where the record goes dark — dynasty trusts, philanthropic LLCs, foreign gifts — we estimate, and mark plainly that we have done so. Read the full methodology →
