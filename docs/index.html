Methodology

The Scrooge Score (0-100) measures how much a billionaire is undergiving relative to their capacity. Higher scores indicate more Scrooge-like behavior.

Score Components:

  • Observable Giving: Foundation grants + announced gifts + securities transfers vs liquid wealth
  • Tenure Penalty: Longer wealth tenure = higher expectation to have given
  • Pledge Breach: Giving Pledge signers who haven't met targets
  • Red Flags: Low payout rates, DAF transfers, high compensation

Data Confidence

HIGH: 990-PF, SEC Form 4, FEC
MEDIUM: Announced gifts, Wikipedia
LOW: DAF estimates, LLC estimates

Data sources: Forbes RTB API, ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer, SEC EDGAR, FEC OpenData, IPS Giving Pledge Dataset
